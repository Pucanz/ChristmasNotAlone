Christmas in Family

The Christmas in Family Project aims to connect people who have no family to spend Christmas with families willing to welcome and share the celebration. Many people face Christmas alone, and we want to change that by creating a network of support and solidarity.

How It Works:

Registration of Individuals: People without family sign up, providing basic information.

Registration of Families: Families volunteer to host someone.

Connection: We link individuals and families, ensuring everyone feels welcome.

Expected Impact: Promote inclusion, solidarity, and joyful moments, ensuring no one spends Christmas alone.
